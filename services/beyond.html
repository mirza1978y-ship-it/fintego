FAQ

fintego Beyond is a digital transformation as-a-Service offering from fintego, a trusted provider of hosting, run, change and support services for banking and capital markets organizations, including wealth managers and insurance companies. fintego Beyond allows businesses to shift from CAPEX to OPEX, reduces TCO, stabilizes costs, and enhances organizational agility and resource allocation efficiency.

As a service offering, fintego Beyond includes:

  • Scoping and transition: Our experts assess your needs based on stakeholder consultations and prepare your transition with security, compliance, and business continuity requirements in mind
  • Application management: fintego fully manages on-premises or cloud-based vendor solutions, from run-of-the-mill maintenance to upgrades and changes
  • Service offerings: We take care of the full range of services, from application hosting and lifecycle management to evergreening and upgrades

As an expert with over four decades of industry experience and 15+ years of experience in XaaS and utility offerings, fintego is trusted by 350+ BCM organizations. Our clients include 17 of the world’s top 20 banks.

fintego is also a top-tier partner of hyperscaling leaders, such as AWS, Google Cloud, IBM, Microsoft Azure, and Oracle, as well as independent software vendors (Calypso, AxiomSL, Temenos, Finastra Kondor).

Our proven track record and technology expertise enable us to deliver substantial TCO reductions and facilitate the most complex transitions. fintego is also a leading enabler of innovation in financial services, with hands-on knowledge in cutting-edge applications of artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), cloud and data analytics.

fintego’s services enable our clients to reduce TCO by >30%, all while maximizing operational efficiency and enabling business growth unconstrained by CAPEX expenses.

Opting for digital transformation as-a-Service allows you to benefit from:

  • Enhanced cost control. This service offering is your opportunity to move from CAPEX to OPEX, making your IT spending predictable and stabilizing the TCO
  • End-to-end coverage. Whether you need an expert in upgrading lending platforms or consulting on cloud migration, your service provider is here to provide the full spectrum of digital transformation services
  • Boosted agility. An easily scalable services model facilitates responding to changes in business needs, market environment or regulatory requirements
  • No team management required. The service vendor secures the necessary talent for your needs and handles retention and attrition issues whenever they arise
  • Latest technology at your service. Your systems are kept up-to-date so that you can always benefit from top performance, robust security and the latest innovative capabilities
  • Improved focus. You can dedicate your limited resources to growing your businesses instead of day-to-day system upkeep
