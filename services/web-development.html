What do we do

Web architecture

Get the web solution with the architecture tailored to your needs and goals. fintego’s web development services span building and integrating web services, RESTful HTTP-based APIs, cloud-based solutions and progressive web applications (PWAs). 

Integrated solutions

Interweave your website in your digital ecosystem with fintego’s web development services. We’ll integrate your web solutions with existing enterprise applications (CMS, CRM, DAM, etc.) and third-party systems. The latter include video streaming services, authentication and content providers, paywalls and ad services. 

Design systems

Unify UI/UX experiences with a comprehensive design system. Our close cooperation with UX designers allows us to craft experiences that engage. We’ll create wireframes, design comps and prototypes to zero in on every flow; define inputs, outputs, and processing logic; and build a complete library of reusable components, styles and patterns for future use. 

Content management systems

Reach your audience across multiple websites, channels and languages with a comprehensive CMS. We’ll integrate your channels with a headless, open-source (Drupal, WordPress, Django) or custom CMS and streamline multichannel content management with content feeds.

FAQ

Web development refers to building a website or web application for further deployment online or on the intranet. This process involves using a variety of web-based technologies and development tools for the front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) parts of the final product. 

Turning to a company specializing in web development allows you to reap the following benefits: 

  • Experienced developers. A reliable web development partner will have front-end, back-end or full-stack professional developers well-versed in your preferred tech stack, architecture, and solution type, from PWAs to no-code solutions. 
  • Streamlined development. At a company with years of experience, every design, development, testing and deployment process is optimized for maximum efficiency. This ensures a faster time-to-market for your solution. 
  • Reduced costs. Hiring an external company comes with a transparent and more predictable cost structure without the costs of in-house development (recruitment, office space, employee benefits, etc.) 

As a web development services company, we can put together a team of experienced developers for a wide variety of project requirements. Our developers are well-versed in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python and Ruby on Rails, among others. Our front-end library expertise spans React, Angular, Vue.js and more. 

If you are uncertain what technology stack will be the best match for your project, we can help you settle on one. 

Before kicking off development, we carefully assess every functional and business request and requirement to get a full grasp of the road ahead. Based on this analysis, we suggest the optimal tech stack that will maximize the solution’s performance and security, enhance the user experience and power the desired functionality and integrations

We put quality and security front and center in every project we work on. Here’s how: 

  • We conduct regular code reviews. This allows us to identify and weed out vulnerabilities to mitigate risks early on. 
  • We keep our testing thorough and rigorous. We conduct security, performance, UX and other types of testing to make sure our solution provides a smooth, secure experience. Our comprehensive testing also ensures the solution demonstrates high performance and remains bug-free. 
  • We stay on top of the latest security developments. We continuously update our knowledge on the most recent cybersecurity best practices and measures. This allows us to stay one step ahead of security threats at all times
