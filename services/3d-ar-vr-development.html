FAQ

3D/VR/AR development refers to employing three technologies in building software solutions: three-dimensional graphics (3D), augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR). These technologies can serve a variety of purposes, such as: 

  • Training and education. These technologies can help users acquire relevant skills with realistic simulations of real-world scenarios, powered by sensor data and visual recognition. 
  • Visualization. 3D/VR/AR can take visualization to the next level, allowing users to preview and virtually try on products or visit any location. 
  • Consulting. 3D/VR/AR solutions can be used to demonstrate prototypes and simulations of recommended solutions to clients. 

Using 3D/VR/AR in your project can help you: 

  • Drive user engagement, especially among next-generation consumers 
  • Gain a competitive edge 
  • Enhance training efficiency 
  • Mitigate risks by using virtual twins and simulations 
  • Enhance and streamline project and product planning and testing 

fintego’s AR and VR developers are well-versed in all popular technologies like Unity, Unreal Engine, C# and C++. Our team members have what it takes to create immersive, high-performing solutions for equipment like Oculus, HoloLens, Google Cardboard and HTC Vive and Index. 

Our SDK expertise spans Unity VR, Open XR, Steam VR and Oculus VR. For immersive AR experiences in mobile apps, we leverage ARKit (iOS) and ARCore (Android) SDKs. 

Within the framework of our augmented and virtual reality development services, we also create multi-platform, cross-device digital experiences. This allows our clients to reach larger, more diverse audiences and foster higher levels of adoption. 

Quality assurance and control are embedded in fintego’s every workflow. We continuously ensure our solution: 

  • Is perfectly aligned with every business requirement 
  • Demonstrates high performance and scalability 
  • Excels at engaging users 

Here’s how we maintain the high quality of our AR/VR app development services: 

  • We follow the latest immersive design and user experience best practices in designing the solution. 
  • We rigorously test the solution for performance, scalability, user experience and bugs. 
  • Based on testing results, we optimize the solution to maximize all key quality metrics. 
  • Our AR development company closely collaborates with our clients to ensure we understand their vision and goal in perfect detail. 
  • We proactively gather feedback from our clients and act on it to refine the solution that exceeds all expectations
