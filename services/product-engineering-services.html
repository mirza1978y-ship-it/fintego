Our software product engineering services

Bespoke enterprise products

Attain your business goals with software product engineering solutions perfectly aligned with your strategy. Besides end-to-end enterprise product development, we can also help you meet your product modernization and digital transformation needs.

Bespoke mobile products

Deliver smooth, frictionless mobile experiences to your users with our mobile product engineering services. We’ll help you engage users across platforms and devices while driving user satisfaction and conversions.

Cutting-edge tech solutions

Secure a competitive advantage with innovative products. Our software product engineering services can accelerate leveraging emerging technologies like AR/VR, artificial intelligence and machine learning or help you deploy a complex IoT ecosystem.

 

Our product engineering process

Discovery and planning

We sit down with you to fully grasp your vision, challenges and needs so we can zero in on the most suitable tech stack, solution, and revenue model. We then map out the project scope, deliverables, and timeline.

Design and prototyping

We follow human-centered design principles to create impactful user flows that drive engagement. Our UI/UX team prepares wireframes and prototypes in iterations to deliver the perfect design for your product.

Development and testing

Our software product engineering team takes over and turns the approved prototypes into a fully functional product. Our QA team thoroughly tests it to ensure its performance, security, and usability.

Deployment

We deploy your product to the target environment of your choice (cloud/on-premises), all while ensuring business continuity and product stability.

Maintenance and evolution

We commit to maintaining software products in the long run if required. Our ongoing support involves continuous monitoring, regular run-of-the-mill updates and maintenance, and feature enhancements.

 

Our technology stack

Front-end development

We ensure your product’s UI is responsive and aesthetically pleasing with modern front-end technologies and a tried-and-true, human-centered design methodology.

Back-end development

We leverage the best-in-class back-end tech stack to ensure your product shows top performance, remains secure, and can be easily updated and changed as your needs evolve.

Mobile development

Our product engineering services involve designing and developing native and cross-platform products for various devices and platforms.

Cloud platforms

We’ll ensure your product is cloud-ready to help you unlock the full value of the cloud. DXC fintego’s partner network includes Google Cloud, IBM, Oracle, Microsoft Azure, and AWS.

DevOps tools

Our product engineering services rely on DevOps methodology to streamline integration, deployment and delivery with CI/CD pipelines, automation tools, and bespoke accelerators.

FAQ

The duration of a software product engineering project depends heavily on its scope. The scope, in turn, is determined by the product’s desired feature set, target platforms and devices, selected tech stack, and its overall complexity.

Therefore, it’s impossible to estimate a project duration without discussing the product first. If you’d like to know how fast we can have your specific product ready for launch, we strongly encourage you to contact us and discuss your product idea with our experts.

Overall, a software product development project consists of five main phases:

  • Product ideation, requirement discovery and project planning
  • UI/UX design and prototyping
  • Developing and testing
  • Product rollout
  • Maintenance and monitoring

We can do both. We provide outsourcing and team augmentation services so that you can choose the engagement model that suits your needs best. We also offer software product engineering consulting services if you require expert guidance in product ideation or tech stack selection.

Our engagement models include:

  • Managed delivery. We offer three managed delivery models: autonomous squads, application ownership, and application portfolio ownership. These models vary in the distribution of roles and responsibilities between the client and the vendor. You can learn more about selecting the right managed delivery model in our guide
  • Team augmentation. We supplement your in-house team with our experts to expand your development capabilities and help you promptly bridge the talent gap

Our product engineering services include the full range of ongoing support after the product’s deployment. If you prefer to allocate your resources elsewhere instead of dedicating them to support of maintenance, we’ll take on:

  • Security and compliance. We’ll adapt your product to new regulatory requirements and help you stay one step ahead of security threats with regular testing and updates
  • Proactive maintenance. We’ll pinpoint potential performance, scalability, and other issues and address them before they become a problem. We’ll also ensure your product continues to use an up-to-date tech stack to prevent vulnerabilities and maintain performance
  • Continuous improvement. We’ll enhance your product with feature updates to help it evolve together with your business. Besides your business needs, we also take into account customer feedback to upgrade your product and drive user engagement

All intellectual property rights for the products we develop remain with our clients. Before you engage our product engineering services, we discuss the most suitable way to protect your rights as the client. The intellectual property rights are outlined in detail in the contractual documents we sign with our clients before starting the development process.

To protect your intellectual property from exposure to unauthorized parties, we sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) and other confidentiality agreements. We also securely handle your sensitive data, including trade secrets, with robust access controls, data encryption, and regular security audits.

With offices across 28 countries, we can also help you navigate the peculiarities of the software IP regulations in multiple jurisdictions. Get in touch with us to discuss the legal protections we can provide for your intellectual property in depth if you hire us to develop a software product for you.
