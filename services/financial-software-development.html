FAQ

Financial software development aims to facilitate and streamline financial processes and achieve the organization’s business goals with the help of software solutions. Those goals can include retaining market leadership in the face of fintech competition, improving customer experience, boosting employee productivity and more. 

Financial software development services can include: 

  • Modernizing an existing legacy system 
  • Automating testing and other quality assurance processes 
  • Developing and launching a mobile banking application 
  • Assessing and improving data security 
  • Developing and launching a real-time payment application 

Software development for finance has its peculiarities. The finance industry is heavily regulated and deals with sensitive data. So, compliance and security must be a core part of every process step. New financial and banking solutions must also seamlessly integrate with the existing digital ecosystem. 

Custom financial software development allows banks to: 

  • Ensure regulatory compliance. From privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR) to industry-specific laws, bespoke development enables banks to comply with rapidly evolving legal requirements 
  • Integrate the solution with existing infrastructure. A bespoke application can be seamlessly added to the existing digital ecosystem to streamline operations, enable data sharing and boost employee productivity 
  • Secure data and business logic. Bespoke development allows for implementing enhanced cybersecurity standards, from data encryption to identity management. Continuous maintenance after deployment will keep the solution highly secure in the face of new vulnerabilities and challenges 
  • Enable high performance and scalability. With the architecture adapted to the bank’s infrastructure and business use cases, the bespoke solution will work smoothly, with no unexpected downtime or irritating glitches or lags 

We recommend basing our collaboration on one of the following three managed delivery engagement models: 

  • Autonomous squads. We fill independent but limited roles with our professionals for the carved-out scope of work. We also manage the squad and its tasks daily, while you oversee the project scope, backlog and schedule 
  • Application ownership. We put together an all-encompassing team for end-to-end delivery of specific applications in scope. You are responsible for end-to-end program and portfolio deliverable and feature prioritization 
  • Application portfolio ownership. You give us complete autonomy in managing the portfolio and associated risks. We take over the project, technical, maintenance, transformation and enabling tasks with the squads of the appropriate team structure 

We can also provide team augmentation services to help you promptly bridge the talent gap. Our professionals will enhance your in-house team while you maintain control and visibility over project management. 

Banking software development services are essential to every stage of the product life cycle, from ideation to end of life: 

  • Ideation. If your product is at this stage, collaborating with a development partner gives you access to the in-depth technical and domain expertise you may not otherwise have. It also allows you to make thought leadership consistent and improve collaboration through co-located teams 
  • Development and launch. Solution delivery becomes predictable and stable with the right partner that shares delivery ownership. The development company can also optimize it for maximum cost efficiency 
  • Growth and maturity. The development partner is there for you when you decide to undergo digital transformation or role transition. They will help you achieve your goals efficiently and with minimum costs by taking on application delivery ownership 
  • End of life. At this stage, development services can help you ensure demand throttling and efficiency and improve the cost efficiency of your solution. Your partner is likely to take on portfolio delivery ownership
