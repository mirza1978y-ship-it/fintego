What we do

Research

We never go in blind to design a product’s UX. Instead, we conduct thorough research to have a full grasp of what your users need and want, as well as your product and business. As a result, we deliver a comprehensive analysis of the project’s feasibility, platform maturity and technology strategy. 

Prototyping

We transform user research and business and functional requirements into prototypes. Our streamlined processes allow us to deliver prototypes and transform them into functioning front-ends rapidly, accelerating your product’s time-to-market. 

Design system

We help you unify your digital experiences with a comprehensive design system. With our front-end development services, you receive a complete library of UI/UX components, styles and patterns to streamline building new applications.

Component libraries

We create and maintain comprehensive reusable component libraries that streamline development, speed up time-to-market and optimize costs. With our custom component libraries, you can quickly launch new products and streamline their maintenance. 

Accessibility

Consulting

We’re ready to share our front-end expertise with you on a consulting basis. As a front-end web development company, we’ll help you implement UI/UX design and front-end development best practices in-house with consulting and training tailored to your needs and goals.

FAQ

Front-end development is the process of designing and developing the client-side components of a website, web application or mobile application. These components span all the elements of the user interface, such as buttons, forms, navigation menus and page layout. 

Front-end development determines what the user sees when they interact with an application and how the solution behaves on the client side. A well-designed, user-centric front-end allows you to: 

  • Ensure your product is intuitive and easy to navigate 
  • Leave a lasting first impression on new users 
  • Maximize user satisfaction, fostering loyalty and reducing churn as a result 
  • Drive engagement, increasing customer lifetime value and boosting conversions 
  • Build trust toward your brand 
  • Increase your reach by ensuring responsiveness across devices and browsers 

Our front-end developers are proficient in a variety of technologies and frameworks, as well as the latest front-end development standards and best practices for both web and mobile solutions. Our expertise spans the following technologies and frameworks: 

  • HTML, including HTML5 
  • CSS, including CSS3 
  • JavaScript 
  • React.js 
  • Angular 
  • Vue.js 

If you don’t know which technologies and frameworks will execute your vision the best, we’re here to help you select the most suitable technology stack. To do so, we analyze your solution’s business and functional requirements and match them with the best technologies currently available. 

We can also analyze and provide feedback on the technology stack you’ve selected in-house to ensure it aligns perfectly with your vision. 

To ensure every fintego solution meets and exceeds our client’s expectations toward quality and consistency, we maintain a rigorous quality assurance process throughout the project. It includes: 

  • Thorough code reviews performed regularly 
  • Automated testing that helps ensure the solution’s quality at a minimum cost 
  • Cross-browser/cross-device compatibility testing to pinpoint and resolve any issues before deployment 

In addition to quality assurance, our dedicated developers stay on top of the latest in the industry’s best practices and coding standards. This allows us to guarantee our code is clean, easy to maintain and highly scalable. 

To ensure our solution’s alignment with the client’s vision and objectives, we collaborate closely with project stakeholders. We don’t just regularly update them on the project’s progress —– we also seek out their feedback and promptly incorporate it into our process.
