Our custom software development services

Web development

Give employee productivity a boost and improve customer conversions and loyalty with a web app tailored to your goals. From online marketplaces and customer portals to web-based enterprise software solutions, our custom software development capabilities can match the complexity level of any solution. 

Mobile development

Boost operational efficiency as well as customer engagement and loyalty with a bespoke mobile application. Our custom software development services include building both native and cross-platform apps for iOS and Android devices, from discovery and UI/UX design to implementation, testing and rollout. 

Cloud development

Leverage automated scalability, optimize costs, and implement cutting-edge AI and big data capabilities with fintego’s cloud-based custom software solutions. We can develop a cloud app from scratch or migrate an existing one to the cloud, all while ensuring optimal, predictable cloud spending and streamlining deployment with DevOps best practices. 

Legacy modernization

Optimize IT spending, improve stability and performance and lay the groundwork for cutting-edge technology use with fintego’s legacy modernization services. We’ll identify the optimal approach to free you from the burden of legacy technology and make the transition as risk- and disruption-free as possible. 

Software integration services

Ensure your software stack works seamlessly as a singular digital ecosystem to enhance efficiency and to power smooth omnichannel experiences. We’ll connect your bespoke and off-the-shelf systems into an ecosystem that drives your business growth while ensuring data security and regulatory compliance. 

Tech advisory

Leverage our decades-long technology and domain expertise to identify the right digital transformation strategy or ensure your systems operate at their finest. Our subject matter experts and senior digital talent are here to help you make sound decisions that translate into a competitive advantage for your business. 

 

Reap the benefits of custom software development with fintego

Functionality tailored to your needs

Bespoke solutions are designed and developed to meet your unique needs, contexts, and goals, from the feature set to the interface, user flows, security, and integrations. As a result, this gives you unparalleled flexibility in designing the solution. 

Lower TCO in the long run

While custom software development requires a substantial upfront investment, a bespoke solution is often more cost-efficient in the long run than an off-the-shelf one involving a recurring subscription fee. 

Unbound innovation potential

From AR/VR experience to next-generation AI and IoT capabilities, bespoke solutions are the way to push the boundaries of what’s possible and corner new niches. Certain capabilities require bespoke development as they’re not supported by low-code solutions. 

Advanced security

From secure data storage and exchange to advanced authentication features and role-based controls, bespoke solutions ensure that sensitive data is always safe and secure. You can also implement security practices, as required by law, in specific industries. 

Total compliance

Custom software solutions succeed at regulatory compliance where their off-the-shelf counterparts often fail as they can be tailored to meet any range of legal requirements across multiple jurisdictions. 

Seamless integration

Your bespoke solution can be integrated with any number of other digital solutions that comprise your ecosystem, from enterprise tools to customer applications, thus ensuring maximum operational efficiency. 

 

How we approach custom software development

Discovery

We sit down with you to discuss your needs, challenges, and requirements, as well as your business processes, to zero in on the best solution possible. 

Planning

We analyze your requirements and map out the project scope and high-level timeline, with key metrics and clear deliverables. 

UI/UX design

We map out the software architecture as our UI/UX design team employs human-centered design principles to craft user flows that are convenient, engaging and impactful. 

Development

Our developers translate wireframes and prototypes into fully functional products using the selected tech stack. 

Quality assurance

Our QA and testing team ensures your solution demonstrates top-notch performance, solid security, a smooth user experience and seamless integration with other systems. 

Deployment

We roll out your solution to your cloud or on-premises infrastructure with minimum disruption to your business operations. 

Support

At your request, we can move into a supporting role and maintain and update the solution to keep it secure as well as ensure its performance and that it has an outstanding UX.

Our engagement models

Managed delivery autonomous squads

Our dedicated team takes on development, analysis, and quality assurance in independent but pre-defined, specific, limited roles. We manage squads while you oversee the scope and product backlog, as well as schedule and feature prioritization. 

Managed delivery application ownership

We take on end-to-end application delivery with an independent and all-encompassing team of professionals. We’re responsible for project deliverables management and schedule while you oversee end-to-end program/portfolio deliverables and architecture decisions. 

Managed delivery application portfolio ownership

We provide custom application development services for a portfolio of applications and ensure end-to-end managed delivery for it. We secure product expertise and take on application management while you focus on portfolio expertise and regional management. 

Team augmentation

We supplement your in-house development team with our custom software developers to help you close the talent gap promptly. Our developers will seamlessly integrate with your team and work under your management and supervision just like an in-house employee would.

FAQ

The duration of the custom software development process depends on a variety of factors, from the selected tech stack to functional and business requirement complexity. While a typical bespoke engineering project timeline is measured in months, the exact duration is impossible to estimate without first considering the project’s specifics. 

Here are the five factors that affect development time the most: 

  • Application type (mobile/web/desktop app; enterprise/customer-facing solution) 
  • Application complexity in terms of functionality, data processing, user flows, etc. 
  • Technologies involved (AR/VR, AI, IoT, etc.) 
  • Selected tech stack (programming languages, frameworks, automation tools) 
  • Development accelerators 

If you’d like to know how long it would take to make your bespoke solution a reality, don’t hesitate to reach out to our experts. We’ll provide you with a project duration estimate after discussing your project’s specifics. 

Yes, we can ensure your bespoke solution seamlessly enters your existing software stack. Whether you need your solution to be connected with other bespoke tools or third-party solutions, we’ll enable smooth, secure data exchange between all systems per your requirements. 

The software integrations we implement span bespoke enterprise platforms, third-party enterprise SaaS solutions, data warehouses and more. With our help, your solution can leverage integrations to: 

  • Enable seamless data exchange between databases 
  • Power specific features that streamline user flows 
  • Deliver omnichannel experiences 
  • Automate repetitive workflows 

The bespoke development cost ultimately depends on the project duration and team size and composition, as well as the selected engagement model. The first two aspects are affected by: 

  • Number of platforms supported 
  • UI/UX design requirements (unique interface design, the number of screens, etc.) 
  • Number of features and their complexity 
  • Number of integrations with other systems 
  • Whether migration from legacy systems is required 

Considering the number of variables that impact project costs, it’s best to talk to a fintego expert to discuss your needs and they’ll help you to understand the financial implications of your particular needs. 

We offer flexible engagement models that allow us to adapt our collaboration to your desired involvement level. Here’s how your involvement differs under our three different managed delivery models: 

  • Autonomous squads. We fill the development, analysis, and quality assurance roles. You are responsible for the rest of the roles, from CTO, regional management and portfolio expertise to application management, product expertise, scrum master, architect, and DevOps. 
  • Application ownership. In addition to the roles we fill under the autonomous squad model, we also take on scrum master, architect, and DevOps responsibilities. You oversee product expertise and application management. 
  • Portfolio ownership. You are responsible for the CTO, regional management, and portfolio expertise roles, while we take on the rest, from application management and product expertise to architecture and DevOps.
