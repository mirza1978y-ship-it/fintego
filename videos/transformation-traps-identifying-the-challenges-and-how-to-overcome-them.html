Navigating banking digital transformation challenges

  

Discover how to avoid transformation traps and successfully prepare and implement your digital transformation strategy. We have identified common challenges of digital transformation in banking and five principles to avoid them.

 

4 benefits of digital transformation in banking

Here are four reasons why banks can’t afford to drag their feet on digital transformation:

  • It enables banks to better compete against fintech companies and neobanks for customers’ wallet share
  • It allows for meeting high customer expectations toward personalization and a seamless omnichannel experience
  • It improves operational resilience, which helps banks weather the turbulent macroeconomic landscape
  • It counters the risks associated with outdated infrastructure (suboptimal performance, cybersecurity risks, high IT spending, etc.)

 

How to avoid core banking transformation challenges: fintego’s 5 key principles

We leveraged our experience to zero in on the five principles our team follows to set up any digital transformation effort for success:

  • Protecting the bank’s financial services in the long run via managed services for maintaining existing systems
  • Building a strategy with baselines and delivering value in incremental cycles
  • Standardizing technology/business selection and design
  • Establishing clear leadership and governance for the digital transformation undertakings
  • Partnering with a digital transformation expert
