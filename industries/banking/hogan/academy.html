Flexible learning experience

 

Our training curricula are designed to create a flexible and convenient learning experience. They feature self-paced Hogan Academy courses over a fixed duration, including pre-recorded classroom sessions, self-study exercises, course assessment tests, supporting handbooks and quick reference guides for each course. Also, you'll have free access to Hogan subject matter experts for questions, comments and follow-ups via a dedicated email inbox during our series of live and lively Q&A sessions.

FAQ

Hogan Academy is fintego’s self-learning platform that allows our Hogan clients to bring their teams up to speed on Hogan in a cost-efficient yet effective way. Hogan Academy enables remote, self-paced learning to replace costly in-person training sessions, all while connecting learners with subject matter experts during live Q&As and via email.

Hogan Academy comes with a full range of adaptable curricula designed to cover the full range of Hogan services, from CIS Functional to Umbrella Application Programming. Course materials are designed to be clear and concise. The self-paced nature of learning allows learners to tailor their experience to their learning preferences and maximize their productivity and retention.

Hogan Academy enables training in a self-paced, fully digital format. Course materials are available within a fixed period of time and include:

  • Pre-recorded classroom sessions that are available on demand 
  • Self-study exercises that are designed to maximize learning efficiency  
  • Course assessment tests that verify knowledge retention at the end of every module
  • Handbooks and quick reference guides that present course information in a concise yet informational way

To complement self-study materials, Hogan Academy also serves as a platform for connecting learners with subject matter experts. Hogan Academy is fully equipped with functionality that allows learners to pick professionals’ brains via email and during live Q&A sessions.

Hogan Academy covers the full range of Hogan services and competencies that you may need to foster in-house. Our curricula currently include:

  • Umbrella Application Programming
  • Umbrella Overview for Business Systems Analysts 
  • CIS Functional
  • CIS Technical
  • IDS and FSS Functional
  • IDS and FSS Technical
  • ILP Functional

We provide a Level 1 certification to every learner who successfully completes our Hogan Academy course offerings. We plan to expand our certification range to include more advanced Hogan certification levels in the future.

All of our courses are designed by subject matter and remote learning experts to empower our clients’ teams with easily digestible knowledge applicable to real-world scenarios. Learners can also connect with experts if they have questions via a dedicated email inbox or during live Q&A sessions.
