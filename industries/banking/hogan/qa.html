FAQ

Thorough and continuous Hogan QA testing can help you address the following present-day business challenges:

  • Rising customer expectations. Customer demands regarding digital solutions’ quality, efficiency and user experience don’t cease to evolve. Meeting them is impossible without streamlined in-depth testing
  • Fierce competition. Technology-driven disruptors force incumbent organizations to digitalize operations and innovate at a faster time-to-market to maintain their market share. Automated testing accelerates time-to-market while ensuring high-quality digital experiences for end users
  • Increasing need for resilience. Rising costs, economic turbulence and evolving legal requirements require organizations to ensure agility, adaptability and resilience. Hogan testing and quality assurance mitigate the risk of costly performance issues while cutting costs and enabling organizations to respond quickly to shifting market demands

Thorough, streamlined, continuous Hogan quality assurance and testing allows organizations to:

  • Reduce QA and testing costs with a comprehensive intelligent automated testing strategy
  • Accelerate time-to-market for new features (fintego’s Hogan QA as-a-service in particular result in 5-20x faster time-to-market)
  • Improve the organization’s flexibility, agility and adaptability, enabling it to respond to shifts in market conditions and demands faster
  • Speed up testing while also improving its accuracy and ensuring software stability, quality and performance at all times
  • Enhance operational efficiency with shorter release cycles
  • Enhance core system security, address vulnerabilities before they become problematic and protect sensitive data against the most recent threats
  • Deliver better digital experiences to customers, driving up loyalty and lifetime value as a result

Our Hogan quality assurance and testing services cover three key areas:

  • QA consulting. Our Hogan QA specialists are here to lend you their expertise in defining or scaling up the testing strategy, optimizing QA and testing processes and selecting suitable tools. We can also help you enhance your in-house Hogan QA capabilities with training and advisory services
  • QA as-a-service. Our Hogan experts can take care of QA from A to Z while you dedicate your in-house resources to more value-added tasks. We’ll cover every QA aspect, from system integration and user acceptance to compliance, functional and non-functional testing
  • Test automation. Our intelligent test automation will help you reduce testing costs while ensuring your Hogan installation shows excellent performance. We’ll identify and implement the most suitable test automation tools, frameworks and commercial off-the-shelf testing solutions (COTS)
