What is a mainframe hybrid cloud solution?

 

Mainframe hybrid cloud enablement involves one or more public clouds and a private cloud running modern applications with seamless integration to a bank's core platform running on existing or upgraded mainframes — a perfect arrangement for safely and rapidly moving data around in volatile working conditions.

And here’s an important operational and balance-sheet plus point. Mainframe hybrid cloud services allow you to scale capacity in line with fluctuating business developments and consumer preferences while retaining the benefits of your existing mainframe investments.

 

 

Flexible hybrid cloud enablement

 

Public clouds share computing services across many different users on remote servers run by external managers (each organization’s data and applications are kept separate). Customers save money because their processes are offloaded to low-cost but, nevertheless, efficient and effective providers.

As the name suggests, private clouds are exclusive services that guard sensitive data against inquirers with no preordained access. You can choose either a hosted private cloud from a third-party provider, or an on-premises private cloud which you manage yourself. Tailored and more secure, private clouds promote rigorous compliance with escalating regulations, although they are more expensive than public clouds.

Hybrid cloud enablement balances the advantages of both private and public clouds.

FAQ

Migrating your Hogan installation to a hybrid cloud allows you to:

  • Enhance business agility and resilience with a faster time-to-market for software updates and new products
  • Unlock funds dedicated to inactive capital equipment, transition from CapEx to OpEx and take advantage of a more transparent and predictable cost structure
  • Boost scalability with virtually unlimited computing resources available on-demand, all while benefiting from a pay-as-you-go cost structure
  • Increase in-house productivity with intuitive, user-friendly, responsive dashboards
  • Meet and exceed customer expectations regarding communication, personalization and the range and efficiency of available digital touchpoints
  • Strengthen existing partnerships by streamlining collaboration and data sharing with partner businesses

Hybrid cloud migration entails the following four challenges:

  • Limited talent pool. Financial services experts are scarce in the local tech talent pool, and you have to compete for their skills. This makes it more difficult to build a best-in-class in-house team without overboard costs
  • Intense competition. Technology-driven disruptors meet evolving customer demands in a more efficient, user-centric way. This leaves little to no room for shortcomings in hybrid cloud migration
  • Demand for personalized experiences. Exceptional customer experience today is marked by a high degree of personalization. Delivering it requires breaking data silos that stand in the way of 360-degree customer view
  • Seamless integration. Building a best-of-breed software stack that also powers highly personalized experiences requires integrating disparate solutions in a single ecosystem

Don’t hesitate to contact us to discuss how fintego’s Hogan mainframe hybrid cloud services can help you overcome these challenges and mitigate risks.

As Hogan experts, we ensure our clients’ transition to a Hogan hybrid cloud solution is successful by:

  • Jointly creating robust cloud governance guidelines. They aim to safeguard sensitive data, enhance security, and ensure compliance with both internal policies and legal requirements
  • Enabling proactive risk management. As a part of cloud governance, we help our clients identify potential risks and head them off before they can affect operations
  • Introducing an authorization management system. It powers permission-based access to sensitive data, ensuring that no unauthorized party can view or modify it
  • Building a transparent partnership ecosystem. We enable our clients to build a best-in-breed solution ecosystem that drives innovation and streamlines development
