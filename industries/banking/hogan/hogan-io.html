fintego's Hogan io solution

 

Many banking operations are driven by powerful z/OS-based COBOL cores. These reliable and resilient platforms provide the scale and security necessary to handle the demands of millions of customers. But today’s digital business world is creating dynamic new challenges requiring banks to optimize and modernize their cores and deploy mainframe hybrid cloud solutions. Hogan intelligent operations does precisely that with a recommended eight-point plan to make the best of your existing core, delivering intelligent operations in an agile, mainframe hybrid-cloud environment to breathe new life into your z/OS CORE.

The Hogan io 8-step modernization plan

Here’s how the Hogan io solution revives Hogan legacy systems:

Mainframe testing

Deploy testing environments off the mainframe to improve productivity and security

DevOps delivery

Undertake the journey to DevOps including testing automation to ensure faster time-to-market and increase the quality of delivery

APIs/microservices

Open the core through APIs and microservices and connect new systems of experience and other ecosystem partners to create market differentiation

Workload balance

Enable access to off-mainframe, near-real-time data to balance mainframe workloads with significant processing and cost advantages

Product/price integration

Integrate external product and pricing platforms to improve the speed of product creation while managing rates and fees more efficiently

System upgrades

Upgrade your Hogan and supporting Z systems to the latest versions, improving operational resilience and other technological advancements

AI/automation

Adopt AI and automation across the enterprise using IBM and other third-party solutions with fintego’s integration service

Resource management

Take advantage of Hogan Managed Services and the fintego Academy to secure a supply of skilled resources

FAQ

Hogan intelligent operations, or Hogan io, is a modern core banking platform that delivers intelligent operations. It allows banks to modernize their operations and bring them on par with modern customer demands and disruptors’ offerings — without a complete overhaul of the existing z/OS core.

Implementing Hogan io is an eight-step undertaking of modernizing and optimizing the existing core and transitioning to the mainframe hybrid cloud approach. Hogan io is designed to address common bank challenges like legacy core systems talent shortage, lack of agility and flexibility and dissatisfaction with core banking system capabilities.

As a result of Hogan io modernization, banks can use the rejuvenated core to successfully and promptly navigate both evolving customer demands and fierce competition.

To modernize the Hogan core, our Hogan experts undertake the following eight steps in the transformation roadmap:

  • Deploying Hogan testing environments with zD&T and cloud emulation
  • Implementing DevOps to streamline and automate testing
  • Using z/OS Connect to open the Hogan core to new integrations with APIs and microservices
  • Balancing mainframe workloads and introducing near real-time access to the System of Record data
  • Connecting the core with existing product and pricing tools to accelerate time-to-market and streamline product management
  • Upgrading core and supporting systems to their latest versions to mitigate security risks, boost resilience and enable innovation
  • Implementing AI and automation to streamline processes and improve operational efficiency
  • Ensuring your in-house team has the skills it takes to maintain and enhance your systems

Modernizing your core with Hogan io can help your organization:

  • Streamline operations with AI and automation tools
  • Lay the groundwork for innovation-driven services and products
  • Tightly integrate your existing systems, such as the product and pricing platforms, with the mainframe into one ecosystem
  • Optimize resource allocation by improving process efficiency and employee productivity
  • Enhance security by upgrading to the latest versions of the core and supporting systems
  • Meet customer expectations regarding digital experience, including personalization, by enabling near-real-time access to data
  • Gain a competitive edge over technology-driven disruptors with high mainframe performance and enhanced innovation capabilities
  • Make compliance less resource-consuming by streamlining associated processes
  • Maximize your organization’s agility, enabling it to promptly meet new business requirements
