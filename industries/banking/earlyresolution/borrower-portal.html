EarlyResolution’s default management solution

 

At the heart of the loan-servicing industry, companies face endless business challenges, including saving borrowers’ homes and vehicles. In addition, servicers must navigate the intricacies of regulatory compliance, credit risk and ever-present market threats and fluctuations to maintain a healthy portfolio. In such a confusing scenario, correctly applying collections and downstream processing technologies plays a central role in improving default management outcomes.

The EarlyResolution borrower portal is defining the future

 

The EarlyResolution borrower portal is an innovative, market-leading software solution for ambitious loan-servicing organizations. It focuses on enriching the borrower experience, redefining how borrowers interact with their servicers during adversity. Seamlessly integrated with online banking sites, the borrower portal’s user-friendly interface boosts contact rates, expediting processes and optimizing efficiency across the entire enterprise.

Introducing fintego’s EarlyResolution Borrower Portal,

a powerful software solution designed to help loan servicing companies streamline default management processes and improve the borrower experience

 

Borrower portal advantages

Elevated borrower experience

The EarlyResolution borrower portal pioneers self-service, enabling borrowers to take control of their situation and find the best assistance options, improving customer satisfaction while developing trust

Greater borrower engagement

The borrower portal allows seamless communication by merging with online banking platforms, increasing contact and response rates while encouraging collaboration

Swift turnaround

Direct interaction eliminates manual intervention, streamlining the entire process, minimizing delays and accelerating progress

Real-time updates

Live status updates for borrower assistance programs provide valuable insights to inform decision-making and deepen engagement

Technical efficiency

By consolidating functions in a unified platform, the borrower portal improves operations, increases efficiency and cuts costs

Key features

Borrower-centric approach

The borrower portal’s intuitive, user-friendly interface can be aligned with corporate branding standards. It’s optimized for a range of devices, ensuring consistency across platforms.

Core strengths

The borrower portal offers a best-in-class decisioning engine for automated workout solutions. It’s tightly integrated with core systems of record, correspondence, text/email/imaging systems and e-Sign providers.

Adaptable architecture

DXC fintego or loan-servicing companies maintain branding and messaging throughout the platform, ensuring a coherent and personalized borrower journey.

Optimized analytics

Embedded Google Analytics provides insights into user behaviors and trends. This allows clients to monitor borrower engagement, usage patterns and pivotal metrics to enhance customer experience.
