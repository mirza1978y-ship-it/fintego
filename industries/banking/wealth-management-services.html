We help asset managers, mutual funds and wealth managers reach and exceed their goals with cutting-edge technology solutions. Our recipe for success includes these four ingredients: 

  • Personal approach. While we do rely on our experience, we don’t offer one-size-fits-all solutions. We invest our time and effort into understanding your unique operations, challenges and needs before proposing an optimal solution 
  • Cutting-edge technology expertise. We’re well-versed in all advanced technologies, such as generative AI, AI/ML for data analytics and intelligent automation, robotic process automation and more 
  • Extensive partner network. Our partners include cloud service providers, business process automation platforms, client lifecycle management systems and leaders in AI/ML. For instance, we have a dedicated partnership with NVIDIA focused on AI for financial services 
  • Proven track record. DXC fintego’s active projects include 30+ core banking modernization, 50+ data & AI, and 200+ bespoke engineering projects. We’re trusted by 350+ BCM clients across the globe 

We can help you reimagine the whole customer journey, from lead generation, acquisition and onboarding to investment approach selection and continuous support. Here’s what we can do for you: 

  • Streamline back-office operations. With optimized, hassle-free processes, your customers will enjoy frictionless, seamless experiences and prompt response times. We can help you achieve maximum operational efficiency with intelligent automation and data analytics solutions for financial advisors and asset managers 
  • Improve investment management. Our advanced analytics and automation solutions will help you select the right investment strategy to increase the ROI of the assets managed and generate income for your customers while benefitting from cost savings 
  • Personalize customer experiences. From tailored tax advice on minimizing tax ramifications to investment recommendations based on individual risk appetite, our 360-degree customer view and AI-powered insights will help you forge personal relationships with customers 

fintego’s data and AI solutions can enable you to: 

  • Gain a holistic 360-degree view of every customer 
  • Personalize product recommendations and interactions based on data-driven insights 
  • Select the most optimal investment strategy based on the customer profile and market conjuncture (e.g., fixed-income investments for customers with a low risk appetite) 
  • Accelerate financial planning with AI-powered automation (e.g., GenAI-powered document processing) 
  • Predict customer churn and identify the most optimal way to prevent it 
  • Provide personalized financial advice in line with the customer’s needs, preferences and financial objectives 
  • Increase the return on investment for the assets managed with suggestions powered by predictive analytics 
  • Facilitate customer segmentation 

You can benefit from data and AI capabilities within a single wealth management platform — our own LXA platform. Designed to be highly customizable and scalable, it enables wealth managers to deliver more value to customers and treat every customer as a segment of one. 

We provide wealth and investment management services tailored to various clients, from hedge and mutual funds to investment and private banks. Our experience helps us understand how your financial advisor, wealth advisor or certified financial planner teams operate — and how to empower them to deliver even more value to your customers. 

Here’s how your organization can benefit from our services: 

  • Our automation and AI/ML solutions can streamline workflows, allowing your financial advisors to focus on building impactful one-on-one customer relationships 
  • Our CLM and KYC solutions can facilitate onboarding, planning and reporting for a variety of customer segments while streamlining compliance 
  • By increasing operational efficiency, our technology solutions can allow you to lower your advisory fee and compete on price more effectively 
  • Our RegTech solutions will help you adapt to shifts in the regulatory landscape promptly (e.g., Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation requirements), increasing agility as a result
