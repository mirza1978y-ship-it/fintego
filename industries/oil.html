How we help our clients

Geosciences

Geosciences

Developing and modernizing software solutions for geology, geophysics and related subdomains for oil and gas exploration and production. Deep industry expertise is assured by subject matter experts with real field experience. Applying best practices and cutting-edge technologies (incl. Clouds, AI, ML, etc.) to resolve data volumes growth and computation intensity issues along with advanced domain challenges.

Drilling

Drilling

Implementing solutions for planning the well, support construction logistics, automation and control of drilling-related operations. Combining Cloud Technologies and Edge Computing approach to provide most relevant assistance in decision making process. Involving experts with drilling background to analyze requirements and check quality of developed software solutions.

Cementing

Cementing

Automate the process of planning the cementing work from a financial, engineering and chemistry perspectives. Integrating engineering and business applications to track and control each step of cementing jobs design. Enabling real-time data acquisition and analysis utilizing modern technologies. With help of our domain experts, implementing different types of simulations, thus increasing the accuracy of planning and efficiency of execution of the cementing process.

Wireline and Perforating

Wireline and Perforating

Designing, implementing, and  supporting wireline data management systems for various stages of Oil and Gas exploration and production. Utilizing versatile approaches to gather and interpret geophysical borehole data such as well logs,  acoustic, seismic and other. Developing solutions for perforation process optimization that help to minimize operations cost and maximize possible oil and gas flow.

Production

Production

Facilitating oil and gas production processes by combination of advanced industrial approaches and modern information technologies. Solutions we’re developing help to enhance production, monitor crucial oilfield parameters, manage economics and automate many other mission critical aspects. Implementing and improving software applications with deep understanding of underlying processes with help of domain experts who have real oilfield experience.

Field Automation and BPM

Field Automation and BPM

Integrating existing and newly developed products to implement complex end-to-end workflows. With help of business process management engines, enabling customization for the needs of individual client, local regulations and oilfield specifics. Exposure of APIs gives new life to legacy applications. Implementation of microservices architecture opens new boundaries for customization and features reuse across disciplines.

Customer Experiences (UI/UX)

Customer Experiences (UI/UX)

Redesigning existing applications and opening new horizons of their usability, attractive outlook and increased productivity. Helping envision and validate possible solutions in Oil and Gas domain before diving into development. Defining product vision and roadmap at early stages, making application development smoother while assuring best user experience.

Cloud Services and HPC

Cloud Services and HPC

Breaking limits of computational abilities for Oil and Gas applications by utilizing advantages of cloud technologies. Consulting on the most suitable setup for your business case considering security aspects, possible regulations, availability of data and application as well as performance requirements and scalability perspectives. Implementing robust and high-performant solutions from scratch as well as modernizing existing applications and tuning their performance.

Analytics and ML

Analytics and ML

Helping to transform huge volumes of data to valuable information that assists decision making process. Implementing Oil and Gas industry specific algorithms which provide insights on the matters hidden from human’s eye or barely comprehended even by experts. Facilitating decision making as well as prediction making on different stages of exploration and production lifecycle utilizing Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence techniques.

OSDU

OSDU

Adhering to and supporting Oil and Gas industry standards for subsurface data management. Enabling unification of both data structures and approach to process this data. Utilizing advantages of the OSDU data platform to speed up new development and integration of existing solutions.
