Enabling autonomous vehicle development

 

The commercial success of autonomous drive systems depends on improved occupant safety, assisted driving functions, drivers' comfort and reaching new levels of automation.

With leading embedded software development, data management, verification and validation capabilities, we’re uniquely positioned to accelerate and scale the delivery of complex projects and reduce the risks associated with autonomous drive technology implementation.
