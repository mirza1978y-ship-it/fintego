Bringing testing technology to automakers

 

fintego has an unmatched combination of engineering talent to help the automotive industry with software testing and validation. These capabilities enable automakers to achieve functional safety of software-defined vehicles and meet strict regulatory norms. We can identify and rectify issues early in the development process, accelerating time to market, increasing customer satisfaction and experiences, and reducing overall production costs.
