Delivering highly complex sensor systems

 

The complexity of testing intelligent and autonomous vehicles within active safety, advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous drive (AD) development has increased significantly.

   

In response, we bring a consistently high level of detail to every testing area and analyze the full technology offering including cooperative systems, sensor fusion, time synchronization, test driving on proving grounds, calibration and test on test benches for perception-level and ADAS function-level validation.

 

Service overview

Full-scale testing

We perform perception testing of analog environment data

Testing implementation

We cover services requiring a partner for IVI testing

Better planning

We help align time synchronizations across all sensors

Future proof compliance

We address changing NCAP and regulatory requirements

Functional testing

We offer perception & ADAS function-levels test benches
