High-quality digital cockpit engineering

 

Digital instrument clusters are quickly becoming a must-have for the automotive digital cockpit. However, delivering such specific and complex solutions can often be beyond the capabilities of small teams of resident engineers.

fintego’s digital instrument cluster solution addresses this challenge by combining our key capabilities in software testing, validation, integration and Agile software development with design in next-gen vehicle technology to accelerate automakers’ transformation to extended mobility.
