Enabling OEMs in the trucking industry to gain greater control over software

 

The complexity of the trucking industry poses significant challenges for OEMs when it comes to achieving true digital transformation. Factors such as autonomous driving systems, demanding operating conditions and regulatory requirements leave OEMs frequently relying on Tier 1 suppliers. In doing so, OEMs lose control of the software stack, digital supply chain and many monetization opportunities.

With our support, OEMs can become more future-ready, with greater control of both software and the software cycle. This can lead to faster innovation and the creation of solutions that align with business, technology and commercial strategies to meet the needs of trucks, drivers and the trucking industry.

 

Service overview

 

We can help commercial vehicles OEMs with challenges that include:

  • Ensuring that trucks are continually aligned with regulations, without having to reinvent software
  • Accelerating software development cycles to increase business agility
  • Enhancing commercial models through the introduction of new digital revenue streams, without turning to a third-party service provider
  • Improving connected mobility, telematics and safety solutions that integrate with other devices, systems and services

Benefits of our Commercial Vehicles services

Working with us enables you to:

Create your own software, command your software stack, and evolve your relationship with Tier 1s
Become more future-proof and future-ready with an improved pathway to digitalization
Create a more integrated, more user-friendly commercial truck environment
De-risk your projects, get to outcomes faster and reduce time to market
Develop more cost-efficient configurations and effective solutions
Address compliance readily and keep ahead of regulations more easily
