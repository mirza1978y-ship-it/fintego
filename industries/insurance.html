FAQ

Technology solutions impact the insurance industry in a myriad of ways, helping organizations: 

  • Make sense of mountains of data. Cloud computing, AI and predictive analytics allow organizations to put all their data to good use. These technologies help draw insights from it and power well-informed, data-driven decision-making 
  • Streamline compliance. From KYC/AML to fraud detection, technology can automate, speed up and facilitate compliance with ​​ever-evolving regulatory requirements 
  • Improve operational efficiency. Intelligent automation improves productivity and performance, allowing for the most efficient use of employees’ time and energy 
  • Power innovative insurance solutions. From peer-to-peer (P2P) insurance to telematics, technology makes new products and operating models possible 
  • Meet customer expectations. Customers are more and more demanding toward claim processing time, customer service and account management. Technology allows organizations to meet their needs 

With the help of insurance technology consulting and as-a-service solutions or bespoke engineering, insurance companies can implement AI to: 

  • Enable data-driven decision-making. AI helps provide insights into gigabytes of data with a few strokes of the keyboard. It can also power intelligent asset assessment and predictive analytics 
  • Automatically detect potential fraud. AI algorithms can continuously scan millions of data points and flag suspicious behavior, all without human involvement 
  • Speed up claim processing. AI powers optical character recognition (OCR) that allows for transforming scanned or photographed documents into machine-recognized text 
  • Streamline underwriting. AI can handle increasingly complex insurance scenarios and make adjustments based on real-time data in a fraction of the time a human would require 
  • Personalize offerings further. Analyzing mountains of data from connected devices and customer activity allows for highly personalized products and services delivered in real time 

Here are three technologies that are transforming the insurance industry as we know it: 

  • Applied artificial intelligence. It has a major impact on every insurance niche, from personal and commercial to life and annuities. Applied AI can enable, streamline and automate core insurance processes: distribution, claims, underwriting and service 
  • Cloud computing. Migrating to the cloud allows insurance companies to shed the limitations of on-premises infrastructure, thus maximizing software performance, stability and scalability. Moving to the cloud is also a necessary prerequisite for drawing insights from mountains of data 
  • Automation. Robotic process automation continues to streamline workflows and improve operational efficiency. New cutting-edge automation technology solutions emerge as well, such as using the Industrial Internet of Things to monitor equipment condition and power predictive maintenance
