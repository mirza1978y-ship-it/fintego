Loan IQ advantages

Cost savings

With Loan IQ, you no longer have to allocate funds to maintain multiple systems. Loan IQ replaces them with a single platform, reducing IT support costs by 30%, integration costs by 50% and IT maintenance costs by 20-80%. 

More efficient resource allocation

With Loan IQ implementation, you can easily view non-performing loans, allowing you to mitigate risks and allocate funds more efficiently. As a result, you can manage more loans or dedicate resources to a new line of business. 

Boosted efficiency

Loan IQ enables you to automate tedious workflows and optimize non-automatable tasks to reduce headcount by 20-40%. The platform cuts down loan processing time by 20-30%, speeds up deals by 20-50% and accelerates calculations by 30-80%. 

Improved transparency

Loan IQ implementation enables you to streamline regulatory compliance and risk management with a single source of reliable, high-quality data. Loan IQ can also help you reduce loan defaults by 20% and make report creation 90% faster. 

Unparalleled flexibility

With fintego’s help, you can tailor Loan IQ to your particular needs. The system is designed to be highly customizable and scalable. You can choose to integrate it as an installed solution or consume it as-a-Service to benefit from lower costs and accelerated time-to-market.

Loan IQ solution features

Loan IQ is the industry-leading all-in-one commercial lending solution that supports multiple loan types, from commercial real estate and agricultural to asset-based and project loans. 

Lifecycle management

Loan IQ brings together all the functionality necessary for servicing loans on the agency level, allowing you to streamline processes, mitigate risks and minimize errors. 

Deal management

Loan IQ simplifies servicing complex syndicated deals involving multiple lenders. No matter the complexity of the deal, Loan IQ can handle it. 

Trading monitoring and management

Loan IQ allows you to monitor all back-office transactions in real time. The solution supports multi-currency and multi-branch data aggregation and analytics. 

Workflow optimization

Loan IQ comes with powerful automation functionality that allows for streamlining whole workflows across all loan booking and processing operations. 

Accounting and audit control

Loan IQ simplifies accounting with robust online accounting features and real-time debit and credit data for multi-branch and multi-business operations. 

Portfolio management and insights

Loan IQ provides you with comprehensive data on all of your portfolios’ performance, enabling you to better manage risks on the enterprise level.

FAQ

fintego is a strategic Finastra partner, with our relationship spanning almost two decades. This allows us to leverage the latest Loan IQ integration best practices and support our clients after the implementation is complete. 

Our Loan IQ expertise spans all aspects of implementation, allowing us to become your full-service Loan IQ partner in the long run: 

  • Advisory: Target operating model scoping, roadmap planning, proof of concept, buy vs. build analysis, vendor selection, RFP preparation 
  • Planning and organization: Program and project planning and management under a flexible engagement model 
  • Implementation: Migration, greenfield implementation, configuration and customization, architecture and DevOps implementation 
  • Post-deployment support: Training and center of excellence planning, managed services 

Loan IQ integration allows you to reap the following five key benefits: 

  • Reduced IT support, maintenance and integration costs, as well as reduced complexity thanks to its all-in-one approach to commercial lending management 
  • Improved resource allocation thanks to Loan IQ’s robust non-performing loan identification functionality 
  • Enhanced efficiency thanks to Loan IQ’s robust automation features that speed up loan processing, calculations, report creating and more 
  • Streamlined regulatory compliance and enhanced decision-making as Loan IQ becomes a single source of truth for all data related to loan performance 
  • Enhanced business agility as Loan IQ supports all loan types, from commercial real estate to agricultural and project loans, multi-currency transactions and multi-branch management 

The implementation costs depend on a variety of factors, from customizations required to the scope of migration necessary. Of course, you also have to factor in the costs of the solution itself, as well as its support and maintenance in the long run. 

That said, bringing all of your commercial lending operations into one solution allows you to reduce costs across the board. For example, IT maintenance costs alone can be reduced by as much as 80%. 

If you’d like to have a detailed breakdown of Loan IQ implementation costs based on your particular context and requirements, don’t hesitate to contact us. We’ll get back to you to discuss your project and provide you with an estimate following the discussion.
