FAQ

fintego’s Temenos health check is a comprehensive audit of the Temenos core banking system. During the health check, our team reviews the system’s architecture, integrations, local development and infrastructure to identify the issues and areas of improvement.

We aim to reveal the root causes of known issues and bottlenecks your organization is experiencing, as well as obscure problems that contribute to inefficiencies.

We present our findings in a comprehensive report. In addition to the revealed issues, we also outline the most suitable fixes for each shortcoming.

fintego’s comprehensive health check can help Temenos users:

  • Maximize system performance, scalability, stability and reliability
  • Reduce maintenance costs
  • Streamline operations and improve productivity
  • Minimize the risk of unexpected downtime
  • Ensure operational agility and resilience with a leaner architecture that facilitates changes and updates

There are six common signs you need to run a health check on your Temenos build:

  • Run-of-the-mill tasks are taking longer than usual to complete, be it during peak or normal times, harming operational efficiency and customer experiences as a result
  • Platform maintenance costs keep climbing, increasing the annual IT budget and total cost of ownership beyond an acceptable level
  • Your Temenos system has outgrown or is outgrowing its initial scope
  • Vital queries to the system take longer to complete, slowing down operations
  • The system architecture has become too complex to enable swift changes and ensure scalability and agility
  • Your platform is a bottleneck whenever you execute changes or upgrades, slowing down time-to-market as a result

During fintego’s Temenos health check, our team reviews:

  • Setup, configuration and solution usage
  • System integrations and architectural design
  • The underlying infrastructure
  • Local developments and how they can be optimized
  • Business processes’ areas of improvement
  • Implementation strategy, tactics and prioritizations
  • High-level roadmap with ROM budget
  • Hardware and software specifications
  • System uptime
  • Security

To ensure a holistic view of your Temenos system, our health check team consists of:

  • 1-2 lead technical architects
  • Temenos senior technical expert
  • Infrastructure specialist
  • Interface integration architect
  • Solution architect
  • Lead business architect
  • Project manager

As a result of the health check, you receive a detailed and comprehensive report on the identified operational constraints, bottlenecks, and areas of improvement. We also outline recommendations for fixing the revealed issues, with specific solution proposals aligned with your needs.
