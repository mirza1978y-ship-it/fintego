Calypso consulting services

Make the most of your investment in the industry’s leading trading and capital markets solution with fintego’s Calypso consulting services. fintego is an official Nasdaq Calypso Advisory Partner and Certified Implementation Partner in the Americas, Europe, MEA and APAC.

How our Calypso consulting services can help you

Strategic assessments

Get a fresh perspective on your Calypso initiatives, backed by almost a decade of our hands-on experience. Our Calypso consultants can conduct feasibility, gap, buy vs. build, regulatory compliance and current and desired state assessments for your Calypso system. 

Process consulting

Maximize the efficiency of your Calypso run processes with fintego’s actionable recommendations. We’ll analyze your current processes and provide a complete overview of what’s working and what isn’t, together with practical recommendations for improvement. 

Calypso integration and implementation

Ease Calypso integration with fintego’s Calypso software consulting and support. We’ll draw up the target operating model (TOM) based on your business needs and draft the transition roadmap from preparation to data migration and maintenance. 

Calypso upgrades

Benefit from the latest features and updates while minimizing upgrade costs for your Calypso solutions. We’ll help you identify the best upgrading roadmap based on your needs and draw up recommendations on minimizing disruptions and maximizing efficiency. 

Calypso in the cloud

Reap the benefits of cloud working with fintego’s help. We’ll assess your cloud readiness and prepare a roadmap for migrating your Calypso solution. We can also assist you with cloud transition via cloud accelerator services and the fintego Cloud Management Platform. 

Knowledge sharing

Empower your teams to make the most of Calypso with streamlined knowledge sharing. We’ll help you set up an all-encompassing knowledge-sharing system to facilitate exchanges. Our experts can also ramp up your in-house Calypso capabilities via training sessions. 

Governance and thought leadership

Back your Calypso system with a comprehensive governance policy that maximizes productivity and efficiency. We’ll help you devise an effective governance and thought leadership strategy or review your existing one.

FAQ

Calypso allows capital markets organizations to benefit from: 

  • Holistic risk management and analytics. Calypso offers an enterprise-wide, cross-asset view of the books, enabling a 360-degree view of risk profiles and trading operations 
  • Compliant portfolio management. Calypso includes regulatory compliance checks and tracks corresponding metrics out of the box 
  • Top-notch performance. Calypso is designed to show quick response times and high performance at all times 
  • Real-time capabilities. Calypso updates its analytics based on real-time data and performs instant checks whenever necessary 
  • Cloud readiness out of the box. While Calypso can be deployed on-premises, it’s developed with cloud deployment in mind 

Yes. fintego’s Calypso consulting services are based on our experience in implementation, integration, migration and maintenance. If you need a hand with Calypso integration, we’re here to ensure Calypso becomes an irreplaceable part of your digital ecosystem and works smoothly with your current systems and applications thanks to REST APIs. 

Be it enterprise resource planning or RegTech, we’ll ensure all systems work as a single, well-oiled machine. 

Your Calypso system integrates with off-the-shelf or bespoke solutions using REST APIs. 

With the help of REST APIs, you can ensure the seamless flow of data to and from the Calypso platform. It can enable you to produce reports or perform checks, for example. 

REST APIs can also allow other systems to control the execution of specific tasks within the Calypso platform. With the integration in place, data or an action in another system can trigger an event (e.g., pre-trade check) within the Calypso solution. 

Yes, our Calypso software consulting services include advisory on upgrades. We can conduct a gap analysis for your current Calypso version and develop a roadmap for the optimal upgrading route that will reduce the TCO, improve software quality and agility and reduce risks. 

We also help organizations streamline upgrades with Calypso Project Simplify. Calypso Project Simplify involves a one-time upgrade to Calypso v16 or later. It enables organizations to benefit from continuous monthly updates combined with a major version upgrade every 2 years. 

Learn more about Calypso Project Simplify in our brochure.
