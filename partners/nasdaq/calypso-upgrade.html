Streamline updates with Calypso Project Simplify

 

Project Simplify is a Calypso upgrades solution that enables your business to continuously benefit from the monthly Calypso updates without the need for bespoke deliveries. It involves a single implementation of Calypso v16+ and an automated monthly rebasing setup. Major upgrades take place every 2 years.

FAQ

Updating Calypso allows you to: 

  • Leverage the latest functionality and features 
  • Reduce the total cost of ownership 
  • Mitigate security and business risks that stem from outdated software 
  • Maintain vendor support over time 
  • Ensure business agility and solution scalability 
  • Avoid software instability and poor performance 
  • Benefit from improved user experience 
  • Reduce maintenance costs by moving away from end-of-life Calypso versions 

You can opt for a single major Calypso upgrade to a later version. In this case, we upgrade your systems, including the database and bespoke code, to your preferred version without revising your updating processes. 

You can also choose Calypso Project Simplify. Here, we ensure you’re prepared for continuous monthly rebasing, allowing you to benefit from regular, smaller updates. Major upgrades still take place once every 2 years. 

Upgrading Calypso itself is a five-phase journey: 

  • Preparing for upgrade (prerequisites check, configuration setup) 
  • Upgrading the application (new version installation and configuration) 
  • Upgrading the database and migrating the data if necessary 
  • Validating and verifying the application configuration and database changes 
  • Deploying and verifying the application 

If you partner with a Calypso upgrades company like fintego, the upgrading process is as follows: 

  • Preparation: Identifying the project’s objectives and scope and establishing a roadmap 
  • Upgrade: Updating the application and database to a new version 
  • Transformation: Implementing new features and functionality for the client’s operations 
  • Quality control: Validating application and database changes 

An upgrade partner like fintego can also provide training for your in-house Calypso team post-upgrade.
