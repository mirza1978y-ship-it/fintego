Specialists in risk management and credit origination solutions

 

Moody’s Analytics risk management and credit origination solutions deliver accurate results, optimize liquidity and capital compliance, and reduce costs. They integrate enterprise asset and liability (ALM), liquidity risk management, funds transfer pricing (FTP), IFRS9, regulatory reporting capabilities (Basel I-IV), and credit origination into a seamless platform. 

 

Leverage our Moody’s partnership — make Moody’s your decision-making engine room

 

Moody’s Analytics provides financial intelligence and analytical tools to help business leaders make quicker, brighter and more accurate decisions. Moody’s integration, thanks to its deep risk expertise, expansive information resources and innovative application of technology, help clients navigate a fiercely competitive marketplace with supreme confidence. Moody’s is known for its award-winning integrated enterprise risk management solutions comprising research, data, software and professional services to deliver a seamless customer experience.

Moody’s Analytics is an award-winning suite of applications that power risk management, compliance reporting, asset and liability management, loan origination, and more. Designed by a business and financial services leader, Moody’s Analytics allows organizations to make better decisions and mitigate risks more efficiently in an increasingly turbulent world.  

Moody’s Analytics suite includes the following solutions: 

  • RiskOrigins, a loan origination solution that helps lenders improve their loan risk management 
  • RiskFrontier, a credit portfolio risk management solution that powers comprehensive business analysis and portfolio optimization 
  • RiskAuthority, a regulatory compliance system that streamlines Basel I, II, and III compliance and reporting 
  • RiskIntegrity, a capital management solution for insurance companies’ needs, from stress testing to reconciliation 
  • RiskConfidence, an asset and liability management (ALM) system that unifies ALM with regulatory reporting, lucidity risk management, and funds transfer pricing 

fintego is an official Moody’s Global Alliance Partner program member. Our ongoing Moody’s partnership started in 2015 and allowed us to prove we are an innovative and reliable partner for Moody’s clients.  

Over the course of our partnership, we’ve successfully completed multiple integration and consulting projects for clients in: 

  • East Asia and Pacific (China, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia) 
  • Middle East (UAE) 
  • South Asia (India) 

Our Moody’s experience includes delivering complex projects of medium to large size for clients in industries like finance, banking, and capital markets. We’ve helped our clients implement and migrate to Moody’s Analytics, as well as test, maintain, and upgrade their Moody’s installs. 

As a Moody’s Global Alliance Partner, fintego is well-equipped to take on projects of great complexity and medium to large scope. Here’s how fintego can help your organization make the most out of your investment in Moody’s Analytics: 

  • Moody’s consultancy: we can help you select the right product, ensure SLA/OSA compliance, and advise you on resource planning 
  • Application support: our engineers are here for you to debug, upgrade, and optimize your system and set up automations 
  • Development: we can implement Moody’s solutions into your digital ecosystem, help you migrate to Moody’s products, and upgrade your solution 
  • Testing: our experts can ensure your Moody’s solutions’ outstanding, bug-free performance and help you save on QA costs with test automation and execution
