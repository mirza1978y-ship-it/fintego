We used our hands-on experience with Calypso upgrade projects to establish best practices for cross-version migration. They later became the foundation for fintego’s Project Simplify framework and toolkit. 

Project Simplify streamlines upgrades in three vital steps: 

  • Upgrade to a compatible version (v16 or newer) and set up test automation (3-9 months). We ensure this stage’s success with a holistic approach to infrastructure, code refactoring, test automation and DevOps 
  • Complete monthly rebasing (2-4 weeks). Once Calypso releases a new update, your system will be ready for implementation. This stage involves automated installation, artifacts comparison, and custom code refactoring. Also, it involves hardware and software verification, automated validation, UAT and regression 
  • Perform major version upgrades (every 2 years). Upgrading to new major stable releases becomes more accessible and cost-efficient with settled monthly Calypso updates 

Calypso Project Simplify allows you to achieve the following goals: 

  • Reduce the total ownership cost 
  • Improve software quality and performance 
  • Take advantage of the latest advances in Calypso functionality 
  • Enhance business agility 
  • Mitigate cybersecurity risks 
  • Ensure continuous vendor support for the system 
  • Implement an evergreen Calypso platform 
  • Establish CI/CD processes to reduce time-to-market for Calypso changes 

With Project Simplify, you don’t have to worry about missing Calypso evolutions, heavy investments in bespoke upgrades or their lengthy implementation. Project Simplify also eliminates the management of multiple Calypso versions, along with old and unique code. 

Read our four Project Simplify success stories to discover what these benefits mean in practice.
