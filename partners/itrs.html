Advanced monitoring tools for banks

 

fintego and ITRS launched a strategic partnership to develop advanced monitoring tools for banking and capital markets platforms. 

Crucially, resolving platform stability, performance issues and the complexities of trade life cycle, straight through processing (STP) can create widespread stress and a lack of control. Our combined solutions provide a comprehensive view of legacy and cloud-based systems to help banks overcome the challenges of this ever-changing regulatory and technological landscape. 

read more

 

Giving you back control of your systems

 

The partnership combines fintego's vast experience in managing bespoke and third-party applications with ITRS's dynamic solutions that address the complexities of modern IT systems. Together, we enable its 4,500 global clients to mitigate operational risks, enhance resilience and maintain compliance, reclaiming visibility and control of their infrastructure and applications. 

 

Solution overview

Surprises during end-of-day processes compromise operational efficiency. What if we could resolve issues before they impact your business?

Download brochure

 

fintego benefits

 

Our solution addresses the end-to-end transaction life cycle, offering various levels of visualization for on-premises and cloud applications. Benefit from a single view across infrastructure, applications and business services, ensuring complete visibility and boosting resilience.
